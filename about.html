About Us

Welcome to Sahara Cafe, a beloved family-owned restaurant with over 15 years of culinary expertise. Nestled in the heart of Tukwila, our cozy atmosphere transports you to the vibrant dining scene of Somalia. Our menu boasts a diverse selection of dishes, carefully crafted from fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. From classic burgers and fries to authentic Somali specialties, there's something to satisfy every palate. Don't forget to try our refreshing smoothies and sodas for the perfect complement to your meal. Committed to providing exceptional customer service, we strive to ensure that every visit to Sahara Cafe is memorable.

Thank you for choosing Sahara Cafe as your dining destination. We eagerly anticipate the opportunity to serve you and create a delightful culinary experience that will keep you coming back for more.
